## 1. What is Software? ü§î

> **Definition**: Software is the *soul* of the computer. It's a set of instructions that tells the hardware (body) what to do.

### üß† Analogy: The Musician & The Instrument
*   **Hardware**: A guitar üé∏ (Physical, touchable, but silent on its own).
*   **Software**: The sheet music üéº (Instructions on which strings to pluck and when).
*   **You (Programmer)**: The Composer ‚úçÔ∏è.

Without sheet music (software), the guitar (hardware) is just wood and wire.

## 4. How is Software Created? (The Recipe) üç≥

Software isn't magic; it's **engineering**.

### üçï Analogy: Ordering a Pizza

4.  **Software**: The hot pizza in the box. (Product)
